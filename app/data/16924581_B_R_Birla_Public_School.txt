B R Birla Public School is an English medium co-educational school. It was established in 1991 to fulfil the dream of the late Bhopat Ram Birla, the grandfather of the present Birla family. The school is managed by a registered body, Bhopat Ram Birla Shikshan Sansthan, Jodhpur, Rajasthan. ==B R Birla Jodhpur== The school is located at Jhanwar Road, beyond 18th Sector Chopasani Housing Board, Jodhpur, in an area of . It has residential programs as well as day school.
