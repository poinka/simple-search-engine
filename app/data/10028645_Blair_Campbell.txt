Blair Maesmore Campbell (20 August 1946 – 3 November 2020)Blair Campbell, Australian Football. Retrieved 2020-11-04. was an Australian rules footballer and cricketer. Campbell played first-class cricket for Victoria and Tasmania as a slow left-arm wrist-spin bowler and right-handed batsman. He also played in Victorian Football League for both Richmond and Melbourne Football Clubs during the 1960s.
