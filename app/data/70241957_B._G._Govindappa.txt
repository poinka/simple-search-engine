B. G. Govindappa is an Indian politician from the state of Karnataka. He is a leader of the Indian National Congress. He was elected Thrice as an MLA from Hosadurga assembly constituency. == Political career == He won three times as an MLA in 1999, 2004 and in 2013. But in 2018 elections he was defeated by Gulhatty D.
