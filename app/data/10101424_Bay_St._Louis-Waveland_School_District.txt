The Bay St. Louis-Waveland School District is a public school district based in Bay St. Louis, Mississippi (USA). In addition to almost all of Bay St. Louis, the district serves most the city of Waveland. It includes a part of the former Shoreline Park census-designated place.Compare to the county school district map: - Pages 1, 2, 3, and 4. ==Schools== *Bay High School (Bay St. Louis; Grades 9-12) *Bay-Waveland Middle School (Bay St. Louis; Grades 6-8) *North Bay Elementary School (Bay St. Louis; Grades 3-5) *Waveland Elementary School (Waveland; Grades K-2) ==Demographics== ===2006-07 school year=== There were a total of 1,624 students enrolled in the Bay St. Louis-Waveland School District during the 2006–2007 school year.
