The Buckeye Sun was a community newspaper in Buckeye, Arizona and of the larger Southwest Valley region of the Phoenix Metropolitan Area. It was published weekly on Wednesdays and was formerly titled the Southwest Valley Sun. According to the Library of Congress, the newspaper began in 1938 by publisher R.H. Vermilion.
