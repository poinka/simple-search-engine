B-cell CLL/lymphoma refers to a family of genes associated with certain types of lymphoma and leukemia. Although named for B-cell chronic lymphocytic leukemia, they can be associated with other malignancies. Members include: * CCND1 (also known as "BCL1") * BCL2 * BCL3 * BCL5 * BCL6, BCL6B * BCL7A, BCL7B, BCL7C * BCL8 * BCL9 * BCL10 * BCL11A, BCL11B == See also == * Bcl-2 family ==References== Category:Non-Hodgkin lymphoma
