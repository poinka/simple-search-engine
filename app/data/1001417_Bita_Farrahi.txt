Bita Lahrakhani Deinabli, better known as Bita Farrahi (, born 21 March 1958) is an Iranian actress. She is one of the most famous and internationally acclaimed actresses in Iran. ==Career== Bita Farrahi studied art at John Powers’ School in the United States. She worked as professional model for art magazines in California, left the US in 1985, and went back to Iran. Her talent as an actress was found by director Darius Mehrjui. She began her acting career with Hamoun in 1989.
