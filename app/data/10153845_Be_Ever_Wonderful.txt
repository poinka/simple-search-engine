Be Ever Wonderful is the third Japanese studio album by S.E.S. It was released on May 24, 2000, under VAP. ==Singles== "Love" was the first single from the album and the group's sixth full Japanese single. It was released on April 21, 2000, and sold approximately 5,000 copies. ===Track listing=== # Love~ (4:12) # Round & Round (4:15) # Round & Round (Again And Again Mix) (4:15) # Love~ (Instrumental) (4:12) "The Aurora" was the second single from the album, and the group's overall "6.5" Japanese single.
