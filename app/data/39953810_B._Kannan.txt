Bhimsingh Kannan (10 January 1951 – 13 June 2020) was an Indian cinematographer who worked in Tamil and Malayalam films. He was known for his association with Director Bharathiraja and was referred to as "Bharathirajavin Kangal" (Bharathiraja's Eyes). == Personal life == Kannan was the son of film- maker A. Bhimsingh and the younger brother of editor B. Lenin.
