Barool is a national park in New South Wales, Australia, 479 km north of Sydney. It is located north of the River Mann and is characterized by high eucalyptus forests. 29 species of birds have been recorded in the park. ==See also== * Protected areas of New South Wales * High Conservation Value Old Growth forest ==References== Category:National parks of New South Wales Category:Protected areas established in 1999 Category:1999 establishments in Australia
