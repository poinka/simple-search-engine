B'z The Best "Pleasure" is the third compilation album by the Japanese rock duo B'z and released in 1998. It includes many of the group's hit singles from 1989 to 1998. With the exception of "Be There", all the songs are #1 hits on the Oricon charts. The album reached 1st at Oricon with more than 2.7 million copies sold in its first week, and topped the charts for more three weeks.
