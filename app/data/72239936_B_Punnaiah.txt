Bhuvanagiri Punnaiah (born 12 May 2003) is an Indian cricketer. He made his first-class debut on 24 February 2022, for Hyderabad against Bengal in the 2021–22 Ranji Trophy. He made his T20 debut on 11 October 2022, for Hyderabad against Punjab in the 2022–23 Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy.
