Bellur ChandrashekharaiahNagesh is an Indian politician who was the Minister of School Education and Literacy & SakalaKarnataka department of primary and secondary education is now department of school education and literacy of Karnataka from 04 August 2021 to 13 May 2023. He is a two times Member of Karnataka Legislative Assembly from Tiptur. ==Early life and education== Nagesh was born in Tumkur district. He attained Bachelors of Engineering from B.M.S. College of Engineering and was part of the Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad (ABVP) during his student days. Parents: B S Chandrashekaraiah & BC Savithramma ==Political career== He joined the Bharatiya Janata Party in 1984. He is closely associated with B. L. Santhosh, the National General Secretary (Organisation) of the BJP. Nagesh won the state assembly elections from Tiptur constituency in 2008 and 2018.
