B. J. Baylor (born September 8, 1998) is an American football running back for the Atlanta Falcons of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Oregon State. ==Professional career== ===Green Bay Packers=== After going undrafted in the 2022 NFL Draft, Baylor signed with the Green Bay Packers as an undrafted free agent on May 30. He was waived during the first round of training camp cuts on August 16, 2022. ===Atlanta Falcons=== On September 15, 2022, the Atlanta Falcons signed Baylor to the practice squad. He signed a reserve/future contract on January 9, 2023.
