The BH postcode area, also known as the Bournemouth postcode area,Royal Mail, Address Management Guide, (2004) is a group of 26 postcode districts in southern England, within eleven post towns. These cover east Dorset (including Bournemouth, Poole, Broadstone, Christchurch, Ferndown, Swanage, Verwood, Wareham and Wimborne) and part of south-west Hampshire (including New Milton and Ringwood). __TOC__ ==Coverage== The approximate coverage of the postcode districts: |- !
