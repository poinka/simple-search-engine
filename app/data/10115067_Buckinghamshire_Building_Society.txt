The Buckinghamshire Building Society is a British building society. As of 2022, the Society has 11,400 Members and assets of £355m. It was founded in 1907 in Chalfont St Giles, Buckinghamshire, by a group of local businessmen. The original name was the Chalfont & District Permanent Building Society, and in 1961 the Society was renamed to the Buckinghamshire. Its principal office (and sole branch) continues to be in the village of Chalfont St Giles.
