Benjamin Blufford Sturgeon (August 24, 1856 – January 21, 1931) was a Texas legislator that served in the Texas Senate for district 3, which at the time was composed of Lamar County and Fannin County. ==Personal life== Sturgeon was born on August 24, 1856 and died on January 21, 1931.https://texashistory.unt.edu/ark:/67531/metapth1117843/m1/6/?q=sturgeon ==Political career== He served on term in the Texas Senate for district 3, composed Lamar and Fannin County.
