Blaibach (Northern Bavarian: Bloaba) is a municipality in the district of Cham in Bavaria in Germany. ==Mayors== The mayor is Wolfgang Eckl (CSU/Freie Bürger). Since 2014 he is the successor of Ludwig Baumgartner (CSU/Freie Bürger).
