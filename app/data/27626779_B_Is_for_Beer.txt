B is for Beer is a novella by Tom Robbins published in 2009 by HarperCollins."B is for Beer" a Tom Robbins Lite Brew It is a children's book about Gracie Perke, a young girl exploring the world of beer. She learns why every adult enjoys it and why she is not allowed to drink it. ==Musical Adaptation== The book was subsequently adapted into a musical, "B Is for Beer: The Musical", co-written by Robbins and Ben Lee. ==Release details== * Hardcover – () published on April 21, 2009, by HarperCollins"B is for Beer", by Tom Robbins * CD Audio Book - () published on April 21, 2009, by HarperCollins * E-Book – () published on April 21, 2009, by HarperCollins ==References== Category:2009 American novels Category:Novels by Tom Robbins Category:American children's novels Category:HarperCollins books Category:Works about beer Category:2009 children's books
