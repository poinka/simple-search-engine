Bryan Gerald McSheffrey (born September 25, 1952) is a Canadian former professional ice hockey forward. He was drafted in the second round, 19th overall, by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1972 NHL Amateur Draft. He played in the National Hockey League with the Canucks and Buffalo Sabres between 1972 and 1974. In his NHL career, McSheffrey appeared in 90 games. He scored thirteen goals and added seven assists.
