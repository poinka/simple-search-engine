Robert Boyle II (born May 18, 1971) is an American animator, producer, writer, storyboard artist, and director. He is the creator and executive producer of shows Wow! Wow! Wubbzy! (Nickelodeon) and Yin Yang Yo! (Jetix/Toon Disney, now Disney XD)."Toon in/VCU grad's animated shows find homes on Nick Jr. and Toon Disney" , Richmond Times; accessed August 28, 2017.
