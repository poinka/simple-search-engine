Böhmfeld is a municipality in the district of Eichstätt in Bavaria in Germany. ==History== *1602: About 400 people lived in Böhmfeld in 63 houses. *School houses were built in 1777, 1839, 1892 and 1962. *In 1806 Böhmfeld came to the newly founded Kingdom of Bavaria. *After 1945 refugees came to Böhmfeld. ==Mayor== *1984–2020: Alfred Ostermeier (SPD/FW) *since 2020: Jürgen Nadler (SPD/FW) ==References== Category:Eichstätt (district)
