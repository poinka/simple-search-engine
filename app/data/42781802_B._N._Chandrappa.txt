B. N. Chandrappa is an Indian politician and member of Parliament in 16th Lok Sabha from the Chitradurga (Lok Sabha constituency). == Early life and background == Chandrappa was born to Nagappa and Lakshmamma on 1 October 1955 in Lakkavalli town of Chikkamagaluru district. B. N. Chandrappa completed M.A. (Sociology), Educated at C.V. Raman University, Karnataka. == Personal life == Chandrappa married Dr. S.T. Kavya on 01 Mar 1992.
