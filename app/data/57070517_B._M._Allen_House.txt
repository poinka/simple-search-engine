The B. M. Allen House, in Boone County, Kentucky near Union, is a house with Greek Revival and Italianate features. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1989, and the listing then included seven contributing buildings on . It is one of 34 I-houses which then survived in the county, and was deemed a good example.
