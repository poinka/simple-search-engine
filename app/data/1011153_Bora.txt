Borá is a municipality in the state of São Paulo in Brazil. The population is 838 (2020 est.) in an area of 119 km².Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatística The elevation is 582 m. Borá is the least populated municipality in São Paulo and the second least populated in Brazil.Estadão .
