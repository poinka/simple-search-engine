Bill Dunlop (born November 19, 1963 in Montreal, Quebec) is a retired boxer from Canada, who competed for his native country at the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles, California. There he was defeated in the first round of the men's flyweight (- 51 kg) division by Turkey's eventual bronze medalist Eyüp Can.
