Oktober 2010 * Robert Daubenberger (CDU) * 1978–2002: Paul Mayer (CDU) * since 2003: Jochen Fetzer (independent) ==Points of interest== * Hornstein Castle Ruin, first noted in 1271, a former Reichsfestung. Between 1818 and 1869 the castle served as an asylum and prison. It was partly demolished in 1879. The castle's chapel is still in good shape and used for religious ceremonies. It contains medieval altars by Magnus Hops. ==Famous people== * Johann Schreck S.J.
