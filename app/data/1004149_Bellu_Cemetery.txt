Șerban Vodă Cemetery (commonly known as Bellu Cemetery) is the largest and most famous cemetery in Bucharest, Romania. It is located on a plot of land donated to the local administration by Baron Barbu Bellu. It has been in use since 1858. The cemetery covers and it is one of the most authentic cultural attractions in Bucharest. == Hours == The cemetery is open every day from 8:30 AM to 8 PM.
