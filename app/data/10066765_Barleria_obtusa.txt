Barleria obtusa, the bush violet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae. It occurs naturally along forest margins in the summer rainfall region of South Africa. It is widely cultivated as a decorative garden shrub. == Uses == In tropical Africa, the leaves are cooked as a vegetable, and the plant is used medicinally.Grubben, G.J.H. & Denton, O.A.
