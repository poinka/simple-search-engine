B-Sidor 95–00 is a B-side collection by Swedish alternative rock band Kent. It was released in November 2000 and features two new songs, "Chans" and "Spökstad". "Rödljus II" and "En helt ny karriär II" are new recordings of the original ones. Hidden track "Papin jahti" is an improvisation where the band's drummer Markus sings in Finnish. "Spökstad" is one of the most rare Kent singles as it was sent out and then retracted. Some copies found their way to the record stores before it was retracted.
