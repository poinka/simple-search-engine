Benny Charles "B. J." Anthony Jr. (born 20 July 1988) is a New Zealand former professional basketball player who played multiple seasons in both the Australian NBL and New Zealand NBL. He also played in Germany and England, and represented the New Zealand Tall Blacks on multiple occasions. ==Professional career== ===New Zealand NBL=== Anthony made his debut in the New Zealand NBL in 2008 with the Harbour Heat.
