B'nai Shalom Day School is an independent Jewish day school in Greensboro, North Carolina, United States. The school was founded in August 1970 as a Solomon Schechter Day School and originally called the North Carolina Hebrew Academy at Greensboro. In 1979, the name was changed to B’nai Shalom.
