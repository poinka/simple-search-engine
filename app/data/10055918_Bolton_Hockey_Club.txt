Bolton Hockey Club is a Men’s, Ladies and Juniors Field Hockey Club Based in Greater Manchester, England. == About == It has over 180 members of which approximately 140 are playing members. This includes players from the 3 Men's Teams, 3 Ladies Teams, 6 Junior Teams ranging from U11 to U18 boys and Girl’s, 1 mix team, a Veterans Team and also some touring sides made up from various members from any team as required. Bolton Hockey Club was the 2nd Club in Greater Manchester to be awarded the Clubs1st and Clubmark Awards. == International players == One of Bolton Hockey Club's former members, Andy Bull, was on the England national squad for 2009/2010 and Great Britain Team for the Men’s Four Nations Tournament.
