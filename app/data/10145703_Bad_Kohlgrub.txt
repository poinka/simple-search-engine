Bad Kohlgrub is a German municipality in the district of Garmisch- Partenkirchen, in Bavaria. It lies west of Murnau am Staffelsee and north of Oberammergau, and is connected to both by the Ammergau Railway. Skiing facilities include 4 ski lifts, 4 pistes and of cross-country skiing trails.
