Brij Bihari Tandon, is a retired Indian Administrative Service officer of 1965 batch belonging to the Himachal Pradesh cadre, who served as 14th Chief Election Commissioner of India from 16 May 2005 till 29 June 2006. He joined as Election commissioner in June 2001.
