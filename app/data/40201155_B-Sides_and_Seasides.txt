B-Sides and Seasides is a 2012 B-side compilation album by the American group The Narrative, released on 4 AprilAlterThePress The Narrative to release B-Sides and Seasides and produced by Bryan Russell at Red Wire Audio. == Background == In 2012 the Narrative started to record their second full-length album in New York and released the b-side compilation, with another donation packages sold through their official website, to fund the album production. The compilation include acoustics, alternate versions of the songs from The Narrative and Just Say Yes. It includes two new songs "Hallelujah" and "Make It Right" (from the touring EP Kickstarter and Nothing Without You) and covers of Radiohead's "Karma Police" and of Brand New's "Tautou", where the band has created an extended version for the song.
