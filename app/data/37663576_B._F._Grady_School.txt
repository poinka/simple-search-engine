The B. F. Grady School was a historic school building located near Kornegay, Duplin County, North Carolina. It was designed by architect Leslie N. Boney, Sr. and built in 1928. It was a two-story, 27 bay wide, Neoclassical style brick building. Wings were added in 1938, 1947, and 1950. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1994 and remains listed in 2012. However, the building was destroyed in April, 1996. In 2022, Andrew Wilson was selected as Duplin County Teacher of the Year.
