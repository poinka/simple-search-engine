Robert Logan (born February 22, 1964) is a Canadian former professional ice hockey player. == Career == Logan played 42 games in the National Hockey League, thirty-eight with the Sabres and four with the Los Angeles Kings between 1986 and 1988. He scored ten goals and added five assists in his NHL career.
