Bersenbrück is a Samtgemeinde ("collective municipality") in the district of Osnabrück, in Lower Saxony, Germany. Its seat is in the town Bersenbrück. The Samtgemeinde Bersenbrück consists of the following municipalities: * Alfhausen * Ankum * Bersenbrück * Eggermühlen * Gehrde * Kettenkamp * Rieste ==References==
