B. Duraisamy is an Indian politician and former Member of the Legislative Assembly (MLA) of Tamil Nadu. He was elected to the Tamil Nadu legislative assembly from Varahur constituency as a Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) candidate in the 1996 elections.
