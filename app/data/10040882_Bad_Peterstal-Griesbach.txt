Bad Peterstal-Griesbach () is a municipality in the district of Ortenau in Baden-Württemberg in Germany. Matthias Erzberger was murdered here on August 26, 1921, for signing the 1918 Armistice for the German Empire. Two former military fortifications are located in Zuflucht, a village in Bad Peterstal- Griesbach: the Schwedenschanze (Zuflucht) and the Röschenschanze.
