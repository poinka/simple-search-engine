"B'coz I Love You" is a song by Hitomi Yaida, released as her first single after signing distribution contracts with Toshiba EMI. It reached number 16 in the Oricon charts and remains a fan favourite at her live performances. ==Track listing== ==Other appearances== "B'coz I Love You" appeared in the arcade video games Drummania 4th Mix and GuitarFreaks 5th Mix. ==Personnel== *Hitomi Yaida - Music and Writing *Diamond Head - Backing and Production *Murata Akira - Keyboards and Programming *Susumu Nishikawa - Electric and Acoustic Guitars and Bass on "B'coz I Love You" *Takashi Saito - Electric Bass on "Stay" and "Ne" *Katsumi Usui - Drums and percussion on "B'cos I Love You" and "Nē" == Notes and references == ==External links== * Jpop Stop Wiki! on B'Coz I Love You Category:2000 singles Category:Hitomi Yaida songs Category:2000 songs Category:Songs written by Hitomi Yaida
