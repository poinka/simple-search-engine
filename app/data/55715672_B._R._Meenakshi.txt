B. R. Meenakshi (born September 20, 1979) is an Indian badminton player. == Career == B. R. Meenakshi won in 1997 and 1998 four medals at the Indian Individual Junior Championships. In 1999 she finished second and third in the Indian Open. In 2004, she won her only national title among the adults and was third at the Indian Open.
