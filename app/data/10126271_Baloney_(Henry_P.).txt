Baloney (Henry P.) is a children's picture book written by Jon Scieszka and illustrated by Lane Smith. It was published in 2001 by Viking Press. ==Plot== The story is about an alien schoolchild who has an excellent reason for being late for school – he had blasted off into space. ==About the text== The book encourages the reader to work out the meaning of a word from its context. The apparently alien words sprinkled throughout the text are actually from Earth languages. ==References== Category:2001 children's books Category:American picture books Category:Children's fiction books Category:Science fiction picture books
