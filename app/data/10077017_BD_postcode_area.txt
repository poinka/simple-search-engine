The BD postcode area, also known as the Bradford postcode area,Royal Mail, Address Management Guide, (2004) is a group of 24 postcode districts in England, within seven post towns. These cover northwestern West Yorkshire (including Bradford, Bingley, Shipley, Cleckheaton and Keighley) and southwestern North Yorkshire (including Grassington, Settle and Skipton), plus very small parts of Lancashire. __TOC__ ==Coverage== The approximate coverage of the postcode districts: |- !
