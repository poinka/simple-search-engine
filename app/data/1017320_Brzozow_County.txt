__NOTOC__ Brzozów County () is a unit of territorial administration and local government (powiat) in Subcarpathian Voivodeship, south-eastern Poland. It came into being on January 1, 1999, as a result of the Polish local government reforms passed in 1998. Its administrative seat and only town is Brzozów, which lies south of the regional capital Rzeszów. The county covers an area of . As of 2019 its total population is 65,652, out of which the population of Brzozów is 7,463, and the rural population is 58,189. ==Neighbouring counties== Brzozów County is bordered by Rzeszów County to the north, Przemyśl County to the east, Sanok County to the south-east, Krosno County to the west and Strzyżów County to the north-west. ==Administrative division== The county is subdivided into six gminas (one urban-rural and five rural).
