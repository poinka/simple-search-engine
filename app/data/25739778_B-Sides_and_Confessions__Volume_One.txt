B-Sides and Confessions, Volume One is an album by American singer-songwriter Jeff Black, released in 2003. ==Reception== Writing for Allmusic, critic Ronnie D. Lankford, Jr. wrote of the album; "These songs are brought to fullness by simple arrangements and a straightforward production... The only snag on B-Sides and Confessions is that most of the songs are fairly slow paced, which causes the last two-thirds of the album to blend together.
