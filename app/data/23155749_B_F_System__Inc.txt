B&F; System, Inc. (BNFUSA) is a wholesaler and drop ship supplier established in the Dallas, Texas area and founded in 1950. The company imports directly from affiliated factories. In addition to selling to independent retailers, B&F; also sells to certain Fortune 500 companies and some of the nation's largest retailers. Their merchandise focuses on accessories such as clothing; handbags; jewelry; and knives.
