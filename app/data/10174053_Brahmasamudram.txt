Brahmasamudram is a village in Anantapur district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is the headquarters of Brahmasamudram mandal in Kalyandurg revenue division. == Demographics == According to Indian census, 2001, the demographic details of Brahmasamudram mandal is as follows: * Total Population: 39,518 in 7,438 Households. * Male Population: 20,120 and Female Population: 19,398 * Children Under 6-years of age: 6,134 (Boys - 3,113 and Girls - 3,021) * Total Literates: 15,164 == References == Category:Villages in Anantapur district Category:Mandal headquarters in Anantapur district
